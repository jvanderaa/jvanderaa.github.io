    GNS3 Tips#

    If looking to connect via Netmiko to GNS3, this may help:

    net_connect = ConnectHandler(
    device_type='generic_termserver_telnet',        # changed
    ip='127.0.0.1',
    username='pyclass',
    password='cisco',
    global_delay_factor=4,
    default_enter='\r\n',
    port=5000)
